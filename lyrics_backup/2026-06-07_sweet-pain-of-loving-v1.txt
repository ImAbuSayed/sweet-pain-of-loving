[RAW LYRICS]
[Acoustic guitar intro with warm, soft fingerpicking]

[Hook]
I feel the fire every time you pull me near
A quiet burning that I only want to hear
You trace a line across the shadows in the room
And leave me helpless in the middle of the gloom

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers while the midnight slowly falls
Your fingertips are drawing stories on the walls
I catch the whisper of the wind against the pine
But all I'm feeling is your rhythm against mine

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth electric guitar swells in the background]
There's a sweet kind of ache when you look at me that way
Like a memory I want to live in every day
The closer we get, the harder I fall
You’re the only thing I want to answer when you call

[Chorus]
It’s the sweet pain of loving, running deep in my veins
A beautiful storm that I don't want to explain
It hurts so good when you pull me in tight
I’m losing my breath in the warmth of the night

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, it’s a beautiful ache, yeah it’s a heavy sweet rain
(I keep falling, falling for the sweet pain)

[Verse 2]
[Subtle live drums enter with a gentle groove]
The coffee's cold but the embers still glow
You’re moving closer and the world is spinning slow
I taste the honey of a promise on your skin
I’m finding heaven every time you let me in

[Pre-Chorus 2]
There's a sweet kind of ache when you look at me that way
Like a memory I want to live in every day
The closer we get, the harder I fall
You’re the only thing I want to answer when you call

[Chorus]
It’s the sweet pain of loving, running deep in my veins
A beautiful storm that I don't want to explain
It hurts so good when you pull me in tight
I’m losing my breath in the warmth of the night

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh, it’s a beautiful ache, yeah it’s a heavy sweet rain
(I keep falling, falling for the sweet pain)

[Bridge]
[Music strips back to just acoustic guitar and bass]
I tried to run, but your gravity is strong
This tender longing is exactly where I belong
No other touch could ever make me feel this right
I’m surrendering to the shadows and the light

[Spoken]
[Whispered]
They say every heartbeat comes with a careful cost, but looking at you...
Saeed is ready to get beautifully lost.

[Final Chorus]
[Full arrangement returns with a cinematic country-pop lift]
It’s the sweet pain of loving, running deep in my veins
A beautiful storm that I don't want to explain
It hurts so good when you pull me in tight
I’m losing my breath in the warmth of the night
